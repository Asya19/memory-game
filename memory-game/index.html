Rules and training

  1. To complete the mission, you need to click on any one card, then click on any other, to try to form a pair.
    If the cards do not match, they turn over and you can try again;
  2. For opening a pair, you get 3 points;
  3. For each mistake, 1 point will be deducted from you;
  4. As long as you have not formed a pair, points are not deducted;
  5. The number of steps and time does not affect the score;
